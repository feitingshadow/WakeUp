In-App Info Page

Wake Up App

Alarm clock with guided meditations

Wake up hAPPy! Be woken up by loving kindness, and start your day aligned with peace.

Recommendations:

These tracks may take you into a deeply peaceful state of mind—use Automatic Snooze to loop the track until you are ready to get up.

About our tracks:

  • The relaxing and powerful guided meditation tracks are designed by spiritual teacher Kirsten Buxton.
  • Background piano improvisations by pianist Jason (JP) Press.
  • Tracks “Father, I Will Follow” and “Spiritual Reality” made in collaboration with Greg Donner.
  • “Father, I Will Follow” includes Lesson 233 (I give my life to God to guide today) from the transformational self-study book A Course in Miracles. If you are interested in learning more about this guidebook for awakening, visit http://acourseinmiraclesnow.com.

How these meditations came about:

Several friends shared the struggles they were having with negative thoughts first thing in the morning. They loved the idea of being woken by a loving voice that would guide them to relax, breath, focus, and align with their “Higher Self” before they even had to open their eyes. The Wake Up app serves as a bridge between sleeping and waking, and aligns the mind with a clear, spiritual purpose for the coming day.

All tracks begin with a similar message and gentle guidance upon waking, but progress into different themes such as mind-watching, overcoming fear, finding inspiration, remembering your purpose, remembering to connect with your Source / inner Spirit throughout the day, tuning in to guidance and prayer, and giving the day to God.
